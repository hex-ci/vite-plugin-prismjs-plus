### Added
- Initial release
- Virtual module `virtual:prismjs` for on-demand loading of PrismJS languages, plugins, and themes
- Support for `manual`, `languages`, `plugins`, `theme`, and `css` options
- Third-party theme support via `package/theme-name` syntax
- Vite 5 and 6 support
